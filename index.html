    About MAXIMUM DUEL

    MAXIMUM DUEL is a webserial published by Geis. It is a Yu-Gi-Oh!! serial inspired by series such as Baki, Kengan Ashura, and Convict Colosseum. If you don't want any spoilers to start, click here.

    If you want some convincing, though: 

    The thirty-two strongest monsters from across the multiverse are summoned to a mysterious arena and treated as prisoners. They are forced to fight in a massive tournament: where the winner will become the new Creator of Light, while the losers must watch their universes crumble to dust. In this world, it's destroy or be destroyed.

    MAXIMUM DUEL has no "protagonist" - while there are winners and losers, you'll follow every single one of these thirty-two fighters throughout this entire tournament, even after they've been defeated. Some might get more spotlight than others, and vice versa. The recommended way to read this story is to pick a favorite and hope they get all the way to the end.

    MAXIMUM DUEL does not follow Yu-Gi-Oh!! card lore strictly: some (a lot, actually) creative liberties were taken and a lot has changed from what you know of the cards. Please keep this in mind while reading it - I'm aware of Yu-Gi-Oh!! lore, but they do not fit this story that much.

    If you wish to support me in any way (particularly with some change), I'd appreciate that a lot. While I don't have many incentives to give as of yet, it helps me stay alive (and pays the bills). No chapter will ever be locked behind a paywall.

    MAXIMUM DUEL does not have a set schedule, but you should have a chapter sooner rather than later. I'll try to keep this (mostly) weekly, though, but sometimes it might be sooner, sometimes it might be a little later. Depends how busy life gets.

    If you're interested, start here.
