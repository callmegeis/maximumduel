    Frequently Asked Questions

    What setting does this follow?

    MAXIMUM DUEL follows its on unique setting rather than the Yu-Gi-Oh!! setting. Some things may be unfamiliar to you or also be added material. I won't particularly follow Yu-Gi-Oh!! lore to the letter or anything.

    Can I make art/work/fanfic/etc for this?

    I don't think you need my permission for it, and honestly, I'd be honored to even have someone ask for it. Make sure you tag me so I can see it.

    What's your favorite Baki fight?

    Yujiro vs Baki in Son of Ogre.

    How was the bracket decided?

    Funny, nobody asked me that ever. Here's the thing: the bracket was decided at complete random. I didn't plan any of the fights ahead of time, and I'll probably be just as surprised as you to see who makes it to the end. Think of this as more of a writing exercise than anything.
